From my pen



To the lover who is also my best friend,


Writing about you seemed to be so easy once upon a time. But now as our bond is growing stronger with the passage of time I'm running out of words to describe it or explain to the world how much you mean to me. I love you. Three simple words we've always used to express our feelings for each other. But does it actually sum up how we feel? Hardly.


We've come a long way. And the things about each other we've accepted and worked our way past, are far greater than words can ever sum up. If there's anything that's strengthened my faith in the God, it's your coming into my life.


You undoubtedly are the best thing to have ever happened to me. I know the things we've faced together in the past few months are a fraction of what life's let to throw at us, but I'm not afraid. I'm not afraid because you're with me. I know that no matter how dark the night gets, you're going to be the light that will guide me home. I shamelessly expect you to forgive me for all the mistakes I may ever make and keep loving me with the same passion always.


After all that I've written, there's just one thing I want you to remember, now and always. I love you. You're my person. I assure you, no matter what I do, I'm never going to give up on us. So, keep feeding me fries while wiping my tears. Keep being the moon of my life and illuminate the dark for me. Keep being the beautiful and humble person you are. And as Winnie the Pooh says it, if there ever comes a day we cant be together, keep me in your heart. I promise I'll stay there forever.
