Colors




If people were colours,

she'd be a bright yellow and I'd be a deep blue.

Yellow, for it's the colour of chaos, excitement, craziness.

Just like the way she is, extremely happy at one moment,

deeply sad in the next.

There's no midway for her.


She is either totally in love with you or totally over you.

She is either perfectly toned, surviving several sleepless nights

or she sleeps away for weeks without getting tired.

She is chaotic, crazy, mad, mallow dramatic, a perfect definition of yellow.


I, on the other hand, represent the deep blue colour.

Blue. The colour of calmness and quietness.

The colour of tranquillity. The colour of depth.

I don't have extremes. I have a midway for everything.

I have layers and depths that absorb every noise, every chaos and turn it into peace.

Just like the colour Blue, I hide all the storms under the calm surface waves.


It's beautiful because when we meet,

We neither become dark like black, nor we become blank like white.

Our blue and yellow meet to make a beautiful green.


Green. The colour of new life. The colour of hope. The colour of freshness.

Green. The colour that gives peace to the chaotic yellow and craziness to the quiet blue.


I vow to always add a little blue when her hustle becomes a noise.


And I know she'll always give me some yellow

when I start drowning in my own depths.


We'll always make some shades of Green together.
